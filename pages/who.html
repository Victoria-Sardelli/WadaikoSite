Who We Are

We are Rochester’s one and only taiko group, formed under the instruction of Masanori Seto and Kenji Furutate.

Our mission is to share the art of taiko, Japanese performance drumming, and to raise interest in both Japanese culture and music.

Goals for the future

  • Become a pillar of taiko in New York State
  • Share taiko with the entire Rochester community

Our logo

    和太鼓 – Japanese drum
